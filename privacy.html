5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Sites use cookies, pixels, tags, and similar technologies to keep you logged in, analyze site traffic (Google Analytics, GTM), serve relevant advertising (Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, Google Ads), and track marketing campaign effectiveness.

Types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary - required for basic site operation (checkout, login)
  • Analytics - measure how visitors use our Sites
  • Advertising - serve relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • Functional - remember preferences and personalize content

How to control cookies: Manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. Our Sites do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, but we respect Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where required by state law.

5.1 Tracking Across Our Two Websites

We operate two websites that handle advertising cookies differently:

  • authoritydrift.com (this site) - our brand and information site. Advertising and analytics cookies, including the Meta Pixel, load only after you accept them in our cookie banner. We also honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals automatically: if your browser sends a GPC signal, these cookies are not loaded and no banner is shown.
  • authoritydriftbook.com - our checkout funnel, where you purchase our books and programs. As a transactional checkout, it uses advertising and analytics cookies (including the Meta Pixel) and server-side conversion measurement (the Meta Conversions API) to process and measure your purchase.

You can opt out of advertising-related tracking on either site at any time by (a) enabling Global Privacy Control in your browser, (b) selecting "Reject" in our cookie banner, or (c) emailing team@authoritydriftbook.com with "Do Not Sell or Share My Info" in the subject line. We apply opt-out requests across both sites.

To exercise any of these rights, email team@authoritydriftbook.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 days. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA / CPRA: the right to know what categories of personal information we collect, the right to delete, the right to correct, the right to opt out of sale or sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising, the right to limit use of sensitive personal information, and the right to non-discrimination.

To opt out of sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising, email team@authoritydriftbook.com with "Do Not Sell or Share My Info" in the subject line. We do not sell personal information for monetary consideration. We "share" identifiers and internet activity with advertising platforms (Meta, Google, LinkedIn) for cross-context behavioral advertising.

15. SMS / Text Messaging

When you provide your mobile phone number to Limit Proof Life, LLC (operating as Authority Drift) - by submitting a form, completing a purchase, applying to a cohort, opting in via a keyword, or otherwise providing it to us - you expressly consent to receive recurring transactional and promotional text (SMS) messages from us at the number you provided, including messages sent using an automatic telephone dialing system. Consent to receive SMS messages is not required as a condition of any purchase.

15.1 Types of Messages You May Receive

  • Transactional - order and payment confirmations, shipping notifications, account updates, appointment reminders, course access details, and customer support replies.
  • Promotional - new product announcements, event invitations, cohort applications, content drops, and time-sensitive offers from Authority Drift, Keys 2 Communication, The Dot Connector Method, and other Limit Proof Life programs.

15.2 Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us, the programs you have purchased, and the campaigns you have opted into. Typically you will receive no more than 8 messages per month. You may receive a higher cadence during active launches, live events, or while you are actively enrolled in a cohort.

15.3 Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging rates from your wireless carrier will be charged to you and are not the responsibility of Limit Proof Life, LLC.

15.4 How to Opt Out (STOP)

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages from us at any time. Reply STOP to any message we send you, and we will unsubscribe you from that messaging program. You will receive one final confirmation message after you opt out. After that, you will no longer receive SMS messages from that program. To opt out of all SMS programs, reply STOP ALL or email team@authoritydriftbook.com with "STOP SMS" in the subject line.

15.5 How to Get Help (HELP)

For help at any time, reply HELP to any message we send you, or email team@authoritydriftbook.com. You can also call us at +1 (561) 726-7003.

Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is subject to your carrier's coverage and service.

15.7 How Mobile Information Is Used and Shared

Mobile information (including your phone number, consent status, and message content) collected through SMS will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We share mobile information only with service providers that help us operate the SMS program (for example, GoHighLevel, which delivers and stores messages on our behalf, and the wireless carriers that route the messages), and only as necessary to provide the service. We do not sell mobile information.

15.8 Privacy Rights Specific to SMS
