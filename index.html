The Book

Why this book exists

There is a moment every high-performing leader reaches when the results are there - but the clarity is not. When influence increases, but alignment fades. When the outside world says "you've made it," but internally something is unsettled.

The Authority Drift is a guide for those who are ready to stop performing and start leading from truth. Sixteen chapters. Four parts. No fluff.

About the Author

Michael N. Fineman

"Authority doesn't come from performance. It comes from alignment."

Over two decades, Michael has co-founded the Les Brown Prodigy Program with legendary motivational speaker Les Brown, built FinRod Solutions in nutritional supplements, and now leads Limit Proof Life, the operating brand behind The Authority Drift. He is a member of the C-Suite for Christ Speakers Bureau and the architect of the Authority Reset framework.

His work integrates three threads most leaders carry separately - authority alignment, identity, and decision clarity. The kind of dot-connecting that moves messages, money, and missions forward. He lives in Stuart, Florida, with his wife and son.
